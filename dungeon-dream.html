Narrative Level

Dungeon Dream

Dungeon Dream is an immersive narrative experience where players explore a dreamlike dungeon. The project focuses on narrative level design, creating a mysterious atmosphere, and interactive staging.

Technical Skills :

✦ I learned how to tell a story through environmental design.

Challenges Faced :

✦ To be honest, there wasn't really a challenge, but I found it very fun to immerse myself in a narrative solely through visual means.

My role :

I tried to provide as many levels of reading as possible. The idea being that a player could leave with an overall summary of what's happening with all the essential information, but that others more curious could find all the ins and outs of the story by digging a little deeper.
