Level Design

Fallout World

In this level design project, I explored immersive narrative design inspired by the post-apocalyptic Fallout universe. The work focuses on level design principles, environmental storytelling, and atmospheric creation through lighting and modularity.

Technical Skills :

✦ I introduced different ways to discover the level, with many paths.

✦ I tried as much as possible to use important design concepts such as the golden path and optional paths, as well as the player's point of view and different reward values.

✦ I used lighting techniques to improve guidance.

✦ I separated gameplay into 3 phases with distinct objectives — exploration, puzzle-solving, and combat.

Challenges Faced :

✦ A major challenge was to suggest a path to take while leaving the player with freedom.

✦ Another challenge was to succeed in positioning and making the player look where I wanted.

✦ It was also challenging to find the right dimensions for each space, without giving a sense of slowness while maintaining an unusually large environment.

My role :

My goal was to create a segment capable of connecting to that of my teammates, and to succeed in communicating with them to achieve global coherence and complementarity between the different segments. I also had to ensure that my segment could be played smoothly and enjoyably, and that the mechanics were well highlighted.

How I organized the level :

The level features three buildings, each with a clear objective and a different way to play:

  • A prison that emphasizes stealth ;
  • An abandoned factory oriented towards combat ;
  • An observation tower that allows you to locate yourself from a height ;
